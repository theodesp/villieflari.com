About Me

Villie Flari is a photographer, poet and fibre artist. She is originally from Greece, and currently resides in Limerick, Ireland. She has a special love for black & white photography, and abstract forms. In her poetry she expresses feelings, thoughts and ideas from past, present & future. She is a member of Limerick Camera Club.

Colours

- Which are your favourite colours?
- .Purple and Pink.
- Mine are blue and greens.
- What can you make with these?
- You can make trees and seas. What can you make with Purple and Pink?
- .......
- You can make sunsets and flowers.
- And pinkie pies.
- And princesses and castles

Abortion

My unborn child;
Unknown eyes -
Flawless hair, untouched skin -
A blurred image
simmering in heart and brain -
waiting at home for me.
